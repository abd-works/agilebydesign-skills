# delivery-estimation — collaborative sizing for backlog items

When the user needs to **estimate effort** for backlog items (epics, features, stories, or thin slices), use the **delivery-estimation** skill. Read **`delivery-estimation/SKILL.md`** and follow its Build steps.

## When to activate

- User asks to **estimate**, **size**, or **score effort** for backlog items at any granularity.
- A **new team** needs a structured scratch-estimation pass before planning.
- The user wants to **decompose effort by contributing factor** rather than assign a gut-feel number.
- An **interactive, item-by-item** estimation session is needed — not a batch AI sweep.

## Key constraints

- **Coverage boundary** must be stated before estimating — which **bootcamp stages** are included (shaping, discovery, exploration, specification, engineering) plus regression / UAT.
- **Contributing factors** are item-specific, not boilerplate copy-paste across records.
- **Categories** not precise numbers — use the team's agreed scheme (T-shirt, Fibonacci-like, S/M/L/XL).
- **Interactive** — one item at a time with team vote and discussion, not batch.
- **Emergent scope** captured — new stories, AC, or questions surfaced during estimation are recorded.
- **Divergent votes** show reasoning — who said what and why, not just the final category.
