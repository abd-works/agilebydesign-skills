## Goal hierarchy (RAG summary — mirrored in SKILL.md)

The bullets below are **redundant** with **`SKILL.md` / Core concepts / Goal**; they stay here for chunk cross-reference only.

- **Nested goals:** Identify clear **organisational outcomes** at the right level, then **outcomes or sub-goals** under them; material distinguishes **major** vs **tactical** goals (**Kept chunk 1**; source slide uses different vocabulary).
- **Start point:** **Why are we doing this?** (source slide: start from the org's existing outcome language) (**Kept chunk 1**).
- **Objective of the practice:** Connect **desired business outcomes** to **actors** whose **behaviour** you want to influence; assess how each change affects outcomes (**Kept chunk 2**).
- **Outcomes and impacts:** Connect the **stated outcome** to **impacts** for **market actors**; tighten with **who**, **how** people are impacted, and **steps to measure and achieve** (**Kept chunk 4**; source slide uses different vocabulary).
- **Goal vs proof on the map:** Keep **`GOAL:`** as the **directional outcome**; put **numeric proof** on **`METRIC:`** lines indented under that **`GOAL:`** (skill notation).

**Stopping point for one map:** Decompose until the **mapped** (finest) **`GOAL:`** is **actionable in roughly a quarter or less** when you timebox; **broader** **`GOAL:`** levels sit above it (**skill** notation).
