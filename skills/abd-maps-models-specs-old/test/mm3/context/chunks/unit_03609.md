## POWER LEVEL

Some features refer to a headquarters’ power level. For player characters, this is the power level of the series over- all. For non-player characters, particularly villains, this is the base-owner’s effective power level, or whatever pow- er level the GM wishes to set, using the series power level as a guideline.
