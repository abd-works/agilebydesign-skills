## TRACKLESS

You leave no trail and cannot be tracked using visual sens- es (although you can still be tracked using scent or other means). You can walk across the surface of soft sand or snow without leaving tracks and you have total concealment from tremorsense (see Concealment, page 244). Each additional rank renders you trackless to another sense type.
