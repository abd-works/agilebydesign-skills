## PERSUASION

Presence • Interaction You’re skilled in dealing with people, from etiquette and social graces to a way with words and public speaking, all of which helps to get your point across, make a good impression, negotiate, and generally win people over to your way of seeing things. In negotiations, all participants roll Persuasion checks to see who gains the advantage. Opposed checks also re- solve cases where two advocates plead opposing cases before a third party.
