## INVESTIGATION

Intellect • Trained Only You know how to search for and study clues, gather in- formation through interviews and surveillance, and ana- lyze evidence to help solve crimes. The GM may make In- vestigation checks for you in secret, so you do not know exactly what you have found, or if you may have missed something.
