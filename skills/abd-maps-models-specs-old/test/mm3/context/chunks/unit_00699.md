## AGE

How old is the character? Superheroes tend to hover in that indeterminate age between 20 and 40, but some heroes are younger, often teenagers, and some are older, possibly much older, depending on a hero’s background. For example, the hero might have fought in World War II but ended up in the present day due to time travel or sus- pended animation. Heroes with Immunity to Aging are ef- fectively immortal—they might be thousands of years old.
