## SURPRISE ATTACK

On occasions when your attack catches a target by sur- prise, the target is vulnerable to your attacks. Surprise attacks occur during the surprise round of a conflict (see Surprise earlier in this chapter) and may also occur as a result of stealth or concealment, allowing you to sneak up on a target. The GM can also grant you a surprise attack for an unusual maneuver that catches the target off-guard, such as an Indirect attack (see the Indirect modifier for more).
