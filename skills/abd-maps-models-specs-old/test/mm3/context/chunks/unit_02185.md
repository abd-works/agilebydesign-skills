## DEGREES OF IMMUNITY

Some Immunity effects are a matter of degree. For exam- ple, “immunity to cold” can range from the environmen- tal effects of cold (described under The Environment) to cold damage, to complete immunity to all effects with the “cold” descriptor. The first requires only 1 rank, and pro- vides no resistance to other sorts of cold effects. The sec- ond requires 5 ranks and only provides immunity to cold Damage effects. The third requires 10 ranks and provides complete immunity to all effects with the “cold” descriptor whatever they may be.
