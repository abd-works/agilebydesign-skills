## HEARING

Make a check against a DC based on how loud the noise is or against an opposed Stealth check. Normal conversa- tion is DC 0, a soft noise DC 10. Listening through a door is +5 DC, +15 for a solid wall. While you’re asleep, hearing something well enough to wake up is +10 DC.
