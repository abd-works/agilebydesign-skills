## REMOVABLE AND COMPLICATIONS

The temporary loss of a removable power does not con- stitute a complication, any more than the result of any other flaw. You can have a device or power-object as a descriptor without this flaw, if you wish, in which case the power cannot be removed or taken away from you with- out a complication applied by the GM (earning you a hero point) or the use of an effect like Nullify, which has pre- defined conditions for recovery.
