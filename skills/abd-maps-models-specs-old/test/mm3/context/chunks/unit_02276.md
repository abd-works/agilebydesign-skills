## EXTRAS

Cumulative: Your Mind Reading adds any further degrees of success to the existing degree on the target, rather than using just the result of the new opposed check. For exam- ple, if you have one degree of contact and make another Mind Reading check, getting one degree, you now have two degrees of contact. +1 cost per rank. Effortless: Trying again after two or more degrees of fail- ure does not require extra effort for you. You can retry a Mind Reading attempt an unlimited number of times. +1 cost per rank. Sensory Link: You can “tap into” the senses of your sub- jects, perceiving what they perceive like a Remote Sens- ing effect (see Remote Sensing) so long as you have at least one degree of contact. Your own senses are inactive while you are using your sensory link and you can only perceive through the senses of one subject at a time. +1 cost per rank.
