## SUPER FIGHTING STYLES

Comic book martial artists often have abilities far beyond the scope of the advantages and fighting styles given in this chapter. Such superhuman martial arts abilities as leaping vast distances, punching through solid stone, shrugging off damage, and killing with a mere touch are powers.
