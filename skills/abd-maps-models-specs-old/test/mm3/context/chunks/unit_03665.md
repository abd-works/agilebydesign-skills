## HOLDING CELLS

These are cells for holding prisoners, usually temporarily, although some headquarters might have more perma- nent holding facilities. The cells are equipped with Nul- lify devices (ranked at the HQ power level) or their basic Toughness is increased by 50%, which option should be agreed upon by both player and GM (both options for two features).
