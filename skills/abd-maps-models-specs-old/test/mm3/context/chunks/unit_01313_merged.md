## TYPES OF ADVANTAGES

Advantages are categorized as one of four types:

• • • •

Combat Advantages are useful in combat and of- ten modify how various combat maneuvers are per- formed. Fortune Advantages require and enhance the use of hero points. General Advantages provide special abilities or bo- nuses not covered by the other categories. Skill Advantages offer bonuses or modifications to skill use.
