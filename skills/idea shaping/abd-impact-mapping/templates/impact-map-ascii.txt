# Impact map — ASCII wall sketch (plain text)

## Instructions (for skill maintainers)

Same as **impact-map-ascii.md**. **Four-column** training wall: **OBJECTIVE (Why?) | PERSONA (Who?) | IMPACT (How?) | INITIATIVE (What?)**, with **`#`** for goal and impact metrics; impact headline row, then **`#`**, then initiative rows. Stakeholder file: filled table only (no # section headers required if you paste into email).
