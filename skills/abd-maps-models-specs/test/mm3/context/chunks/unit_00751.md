## REDUCING ABILITIES

You can also lower one or more of your character’s ability ranks from the starting value of 0. Each rank you lower an ability gives you an additional two power points to spend elsewhere. You cannot lower an ability rank below –5, which is itself a serious deficiency. Ability Cost = 2 power points per +1 to an ability rank. Gain 2 bonus power points per -1 to an ability rank.
