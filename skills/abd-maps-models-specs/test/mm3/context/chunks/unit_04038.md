## CONFLICTS

A conflict is when two or more characters go up against each other, typically in a fight of some sort. Conflict between heroes and villains is a prime part of MUTANTS & MASTERMINDS and a big element of the fun, just like the colorful and spec- tacular fights in the superhero comic books.
