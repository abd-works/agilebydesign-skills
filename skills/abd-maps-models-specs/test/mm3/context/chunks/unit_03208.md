## INVENTING

Characters with the Inventor advantage can create inven- tions, temporary devices. To create an invention, the in- ventor defines its effects and its cost in power points. This cost is used for the necessary skill checks, and determines the time required to create the invention. Inventions are subject to the same power level limits as other effects in the series.
