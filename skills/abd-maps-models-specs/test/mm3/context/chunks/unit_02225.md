## FLAWS

Absent Strength: This flaw applies only to rank 1 Insub- stantial and removes your effective Strength while in that form, leaving you with limited ability to affect the physical world like the higher ranks of the effect. Flat –1 point. Permanent: You are always Insubstantial; you cannot as- sume solid form, although your Insubstantial effect can still be Nullified unless it is also Innate. +0 cost per rank.
