## REVIVE

You can remove the dazed or stunned conditions from a subject (see Conditions in the Action & Adventure chapter). The check to revive is a standard action. A suc- cessful check removes the condition. Other conditions the patient may have remain, so reviving someone inca- pacitated due to fatigue still leaves the patient exhaust- ed, for example, while awakening someone incapacitat- ed due to damage still leaves the patient staggered.
