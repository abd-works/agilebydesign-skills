## DYNAMIC ALTERNATE EFFECT

An effect with this modifier has a different resistance than usual. The resistance check difficulty class remains the same, only the resistance differs. If the change is to a generally lower (and therefore more advantageous) re- sistance, this extra increases cost per rank by +1. If, in the GM’s opinion, there is no real increase in effectiveness, just a chance to the resistance, it has a net modifier of +0. For 2 power points an Alternate Effect is dynamic; it can share power points with other Dynamic Alternate Ef- fects, allowing them all to operate at the same time, but at reduced effectiveness (so you must have two Dynamic Alternate Effects for this option to be useful). You decide how many power points are allocated to the effects once per turn as a free action. Making the base effect of an array Dynamic requires 1 power point.
