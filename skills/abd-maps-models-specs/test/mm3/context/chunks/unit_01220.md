## TECHNOLOGY

Most routine operations of technological equipment don’t require a skill check and can be done untrained. Us- ing an unfamiliar device does require a check, with the DC determined by how foreign or unusual the device is, from simple (DC 10) to highly advanced (DC 25 or more).
