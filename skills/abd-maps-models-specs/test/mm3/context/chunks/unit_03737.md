## ABILITIES

Constructs have no Stamina, because they are not living beings. Constructs do not recover from damage; they must be repaired instead. Constructs are immune to ef- fects permitting Fortitude resistance checks unless the ef- fect works on objects. Constructs also have either no Intel- lect and Presence ranks or no Strength and Agility ranks. These qualities of constructs: lacking three abilities (–30 points) and Immunity to Fortitude Effects (30 points) aver- age out to 0 points. Constructs without Intellect and Presence are automa- tons, operating on simple instinct or programmed in- structions. They are immune to Will effects and interac- tion skills and automatically fail Intellect and Presence checks.
