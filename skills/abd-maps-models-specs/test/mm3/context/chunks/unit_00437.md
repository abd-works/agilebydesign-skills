## RESISTANCE

Gain an immediate additional resistance check against an ongoing effect. If you’re compelled or controlled, the fa- tigue from the extra effort doesn’t affect you until you’re free of the effect; this is so you can’t resist yourself to ex- haustion as a way of avoiding being controlled!
