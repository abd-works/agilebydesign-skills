## WEAPONS

Weapons are common devices, ranging from super-pow- ered versions of ordinary weapons like swords, bows, or guns (see Enhanced Equipment) to more exotic weap- ons like magic wands or alien power rings. A weapon device usually has one or more attack effects but may provide virtually any effect the player wants to include. Weapons often have several different attacks as Alternate Effects. One example is an array of magic rings, each with its own effect, but only usable one at a time.
