## MELEE WEAPONS

Melee weapons are hand-held close combat weapons. They typically have a Strength-based Damage effect (see the Damage effect in the Powers chapter), adding the wielder’s Strength rank to the weapon’s damage rank. Or- dinary melee weapons are limited by their Toughness in terms of the amount of Strength they can add.
