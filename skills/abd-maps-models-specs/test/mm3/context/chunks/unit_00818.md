## ABSENT ABILITIES

Rather than having a rank of –5 in a given ability, some things or creatures actually lack an ability altogether. These beings automatically fail any check requiring the absent ability. The additional effects of an absent ability are as follows:

Strength: A creature with no Strength is incapable of exerting any physical force, either because it has no physical form (like an incorporeal ghost) or simply can’t move (like a tree). Stamina: A creature with no Stamina has no physical body (like a ghost) or is not a living being (such as a robot or other construct).
