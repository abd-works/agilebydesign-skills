## RECOVER

You can spend a hero point to recover faster. A hero point allows you to immediately remove a dazed, fatigued, or stunned condition, without taking an action. Among oth- er things, this option allows you to use extra effort (pre- viously) without suffering any fatigue. Spending a hero point to recover also lets you convert an exhausted condi- tion into a fatigued condition.
