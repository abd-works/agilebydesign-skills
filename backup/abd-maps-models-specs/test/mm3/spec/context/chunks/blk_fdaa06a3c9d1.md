+0 OR +1 COST PER RANK

This extra allows you to give someone else use of a per-
sonal  effect.  You  must  touch  the  subject  as  a  standard
action, and they have control over their use of the effect,
although you can withdraw it when you wish as a free ac-
tion. If you are unable to maintain the effect, it stops work-
ing,  even  if  someone  else  is  using  it.  Both  you  and  your
subject(s) can use the effect simultaneously.

A flat-value flaw cannot reduce an effect or power’s final
cost below 1 power point.

If the effect Affects Only Others, and not you, it has a net
modifier of +0.
