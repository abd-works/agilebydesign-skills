MIND READING AND DECEPTION

If you can interact with your subject, a successful Decep-
tion  check  against  the  target’s  Insight  check  causes  the
subject to consciously think about a particular piece of in-
formation you’re looking for, such as a password or name,
allowing you to pluck it from the subject’s mind with sur-
face thoughts contact.
