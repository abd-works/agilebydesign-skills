WOLFEN

The  origins  of  the  Wolfen  are  unknown,  but  their  pack
once ranged up and down the west coast of America be-
fore they were trapped inside Avison with Lord Sanguine.
They  respect  only  strength,  constantly  challenging  both
outsiders and one another to prove themselves one of the
worthy... and not prey.
