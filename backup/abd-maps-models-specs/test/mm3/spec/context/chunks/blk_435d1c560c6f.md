OUTCOME

This  scene  needs  to  climax  with  the  heroes  licking  their
wounds, and having developed a consequentially healthy
respect for the power they now face. It’s critical the bad
guys not be made to look weak at this early juncture. At
the first hint of the battle turning against the villains, have
Choshech grab Marmo-Set and (Extended) Teleport them
both away. The cackling Marmo-Set exits with a sneering,
“I  shall  return,  more  powerful  than  you  can  imagine—and
you so-called heroes have shown me the place of power!”

Whether  the  heroes  come  to  him  first  or  he  surprises
them, Dr. Simian’s entrance is the segue to Scene 3. The
Gamemaster needs to make sure his presence is noted be-
fore the heroes depart Blackstone Island.
