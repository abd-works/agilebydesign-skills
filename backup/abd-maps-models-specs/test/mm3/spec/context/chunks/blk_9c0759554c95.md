PSYCHIC

Psychic  heroes  have  a  wide  variety  of  psionic  powers
available to them, but they typically concentrate on one
category of powers, be they telepathic or telekinetic in or-
igin. Within those broad categories, the Psychic can usual-
ly perform a number of powerful feats. Another common
type of Psychic is the ninja-like combatant who combines
stealth and mental powers to devastating effect.
