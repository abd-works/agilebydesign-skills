VULNERABLE AND DEFENSELESS

Two conditions strongly affect your defenses. When you
are  vulnerable,  your  active  defense  ranks  are  halved
(round  up  fractions).  So  the  aforementioned  hero  with
Parry  11  and  Dodge  9  would  have  ranks  of  Parry  6  and
Dodge 5 while vulnerable.

When  you  are  defenseless,  your  active  defense  ranks
are  reduced  to  zero, meaning  the  base  difficulty  class  to
hit you is just 10! What’s more, attackers can make attack
checks against defenseless targets as routine checks (see
Routine  Checks  in  The  Basics  chapter),  meaning  a  hit
is guaranteed with an attack bonus of 0 or more, unless
there are other modifiers affecting the check.
