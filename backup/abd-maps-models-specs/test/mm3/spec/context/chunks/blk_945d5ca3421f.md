INFIRMARY

An infirmary consists of hospital beds and equipment for
the full use of the Treatment skill. An infirmary can provide
treatment for a number of characters equal to the base’s
power level at one time and it can be assumed to have the
necessary  facilities  to  handle  any  unusual  physiology  of
the base’s owner(s).
