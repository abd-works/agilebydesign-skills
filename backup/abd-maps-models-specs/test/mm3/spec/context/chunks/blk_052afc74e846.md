RADIATION

Radiation  in  the  comic  books  often  causes  mutations  or
triggers latent powers in those exposed to it rather than
simply  causing  radiation  sickness.  Exposure  to  radiation
(especially exotic or alien radiation) may be an excellent
opportunity for a complication.

like a disease: The victim makes an initial Fortitude check
