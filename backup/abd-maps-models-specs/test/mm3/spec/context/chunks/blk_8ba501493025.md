EFFECT

A  headquarters  can  be  given  any  appropriate  power  ef-
fect as a feature with the Gamemaster’s approval. The ef-
fect  cannot  have  a  total  cost  greater  than  twice  the  HQ
power level and cannot exceed the power level limits. Ef-
fects are assumed to affect either the headquarters or its
occupants, if they do both, apply the Affects Others modi-
fier, or take them as separate features.
