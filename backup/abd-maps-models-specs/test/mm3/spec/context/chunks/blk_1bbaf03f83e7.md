OUTCOME

Unless the heroes purposefully delayed during their time
in Avison, it will be the afternoon before the concert, with
time to return the Fire of the Sun and Moon to the Native
American Cultural Center just hours before the Longbowz
show up for their concert.

Two heroes must act together, and when they press the
Fire  of  the  Sun  and  Moon  to  Sanguine  it  affixes  to  San-
guine—and  the  heroes—as  if  welded  in  place,  glowing
ever  brighter  as  Doctor  Clock’s  ritual  causes  Sanguine’s
spell to reverse, his minions drawn back into Avison as the
people of Emerald City reappear in the streets. If Blackbird
and Kestrel haven’t been defeated the heroes need to de-
fend themselves for three rounds before the Fire erupts in
one  last  blinding  flash…as  Sanguine  disappears,  his  fol-
lowers fading away like morning mist.
