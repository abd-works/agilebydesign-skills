GROUND VEHICLES

Most cars include such standard features as air condition-
ing, air bags, antilock brakes, cruise control, keyless entry,
and an AM/FM radio with CD player. Luxury vehicles often
also  include  extras  such  as  power  seats,  leather  uphol-
stery, and a sunroof.

Cars  come  in  many  different  varieties.  For  variations  on
the base model, add +1 or +2 Str for larger, full-sized, cars,
and +1 or even +2 Speed for sports and racing cars.
