WITCHWINGS

Birds  transformed  into  the  loyal  followers  of  the  witch-
woman  Blackbird,  witchwings  have  avian  instincts  but
also  the  cunning  of  their  mistress. They  always  attack  in
groups using Mental Communication to coordinate their
efforts,  the  first  Witchwing  using  advantages  like  Daze
and Fascinate or their Sap Vigor spell to set up a target for
a full on attack by another Witchwing using Mental Blast
or Net of Thorns.
