TACTICS

The Bola uses his trick weapons to set up victims for his
teammates’ attacks. If a hero targets him in retaliation, the
Bola looks out for his own safety first and switches focus
to his attacker. Should a battle turn against him, he grabs
the  nearest  attractive  female  hostage  and  flees,  but  any
threats are merely bluffs—he’d never harm a mamacita.

Throughout  a  battle,  the  Firebug  remains  airborne  and
blasts any opportune target with his flamethrowers. Like
his Guild cohorts, the Firebug flees from a losing fight. He
makes sure, however, to set as many buildings as possible
on fire as he runs.
