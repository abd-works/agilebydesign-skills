OPPORTUNITIES

Recognizing that Emerald City needs heroes, local philan-
thropist Maximillian Mars, billionaire CEO of MarsTech, has
put his wealth, resources, and influence behind an effort to
cultivate  and  support  superheroes  in  the  City  of  Destiny.
For  those  willing  to  take  up  the  challenge  awaits  a  high-
tech  headquarters,  online  information,  and  the  grateful
assistance of the Emerald City Police Department and the
American  Elite  Government  Intervention  Service  (AEGIS).
Emerald City needs heroes! Will you answer the call?
