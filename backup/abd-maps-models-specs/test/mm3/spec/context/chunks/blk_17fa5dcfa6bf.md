COMBAT

As the heroes pursue Lord Sanguine into Emerald City, his
followers continue to appear in his footsteps, thousands
of them exploring and looting the empty city. Still, if the
heroes use any degree of stealth it shouldn’t be difficult to
sneak past them, as there are only a dozen Blood Monks
on a street normally filled by a thousand pedestrians.
