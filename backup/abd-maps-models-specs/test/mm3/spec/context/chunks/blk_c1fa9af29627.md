COMBAT

If you successfully trick an opponent (see Trick under De-
ception in the Skills chapter), you can redirect a missed
attack against you from that opponent at another target
as a reaction. The new target must be adjacent to you and
within range of the attack. The attacker makes a new at-
tack check with the same modifiers as the first against the
new target.
