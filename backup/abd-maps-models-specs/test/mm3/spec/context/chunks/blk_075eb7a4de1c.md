TACTICS

The Wolfen  are  an  offshoot  of  the  Beast  People  and  the
Cryptid  Clans,  driven  wild  from  wandering  the  mists  of
Avison. Ever eager to prove their strength and courage, in
a fight they’ll attack the strongest-looking foe first, mul-
tiple Wolfen attacking a target at once. Any farmers who
manage  to  disengage  from  the  Wolfen  attacking  them
will fall back toward a stone wall at the edge of the field,
trying to gather and hold their ground.
