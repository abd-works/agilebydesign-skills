CRITICAL HITS

When you make an attack check and get a natural 20 (the
d20 actually shows 20), you automatically hit, regardless
of  your  target’s  defense,  and  you  score  a  threat. The  hit
might also be a critical hit (sometimes called a “crit”). To
find out if it’s a critical hit, determine if the attack check
total is equal to or greater than the target’s defense. If so,
it is a critical hit. If not, the attack still hits, but as a normal
attack, not a critical.
