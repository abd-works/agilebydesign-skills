ABILITIES

Roll 1d20 once and record the result.

1-7

Demon: You are a creature from the nether realms,
such as a hellspawn, revenant, or incubus.

8-14

Vampire: You are an undead creature that survives
by preying on others and you possess supernatural
powers.

15-20 Werewolf: You have features of both man and wolf.
