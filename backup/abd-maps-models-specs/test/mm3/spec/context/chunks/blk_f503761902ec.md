TACTICS

As the battle progresses, water spreads across the cavern
and starts to rise, becoming six inches deep in two rounds
(rank -5) and increasing one additional rank in depth dur-
ing each three subsequent rounds. The water is salt wa-
ter—the  result  of  the  tide  rising  outside  the  island  and
pushing into the caves. Heroes in the water are Hindered
once the water is 3 feet (rank -3) or more feet deep, unless
their  powers  allow  them  free  movement  in  water.  Boul-
ders  scattered  across  the  cavern  floor  allows  the Wolfen
master to stay (and fight) above the water level, leaping
from one to another and then onto his opponent. Heroes
who  attempt  to  do  the  same  need  to  make  Acrobatics,
Athletics, or Agility checks (DC 15) to keep up.
