ILLUSIONARY EFFECTS

Illusions  have  no  substance  and  cannot  have  any  real-
world  effect.  So  they  cannot  provide  illumination,  nutri-
tion, warmth, or the like (although they can provide the
sensations of these things). Likewise, an illusory wall only
prevents people from moving through an area so long as
they believe it’s real, and an illusory bridge or floor is re-
vealed as false as soon as someone tries to walk across it,
and falls through it!
