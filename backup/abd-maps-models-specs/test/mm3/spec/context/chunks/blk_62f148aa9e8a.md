RESTRICTED EQUIPMENT

The Gamemaster may rule some equipment is simply not
available  or  that  characters  must  pay  for  an  additional
Feature (or more) in order to have it. This may include cer-
tain kinds of weapons, vehicles, and anything else the GM
feels should be restricted in the series.
