CHOSHECH

The already nervous Jeremiah had been frightened by a
sudden  blast  of  noise  from  a  rude  neighbor’s  enormous
car sound system. One clumsy fall from a step-ladder lat-
er, he wound up in Trinity Hospital with a broken finger.

There he encountered Mr. Infamy, though his previous cli-
ents could scarcely have recognized him

Jeremiah could do nothing else but agree, and was equal-
ly bereft of resistance when Infamy offered to make him
just such an instrument of apocalypse. With his self-righ-
teousness  intact,  Jeremiah  eagerly  took  on  his  share  of
bringing about the end of the world in the form of plague
number nine.
