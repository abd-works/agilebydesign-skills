RANK 2 - GASEOUS

You become a cloud of gas or fine particles. You have no
effective Strength in gaseous form, but have Immunity to
Physical Damage. Energy and area attacks still affect you
normally. You  can  flow  through  any  opening  that  is  not
airtight. You can use your various other effects normally.
