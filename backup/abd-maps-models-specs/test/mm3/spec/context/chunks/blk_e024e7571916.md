The heroes could also be flagged down by a worried
person who tells them about the attack on the Plaza
after being alerted via his or her smartphone or radio.

If the heroes monitor the media, even in their secret
ID, they hear about the attack via any number of sourc-
es—live TV  and  radio  reports,  websites,  phone  calls/
texts from concerned friends, or social media. Even if
a hero is not asked outright to get involved, he or she
should at least be curious and start looking into things.

Other  openings  are  possible,  depending  on  what’s  al-
ready happened in your series.
