4 RANKS

A sense with this trait is unaffected by concealment from
obstacles (rather than Concealment effects). So vision that
Penetrates  Concealment  sees  right  through  opaque  ob-
jects,  for  example,  and  hearing  that  Penetrates  Conceal-
ment is unaffected by sound-proofing or intervening ma-
terials, and so forth.
