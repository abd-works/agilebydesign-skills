Street  informants  are  the  eyes  and  ears  of  the  under-
world. They aren’t usually involved in anything illegal, but
they know what’s going on. They’re not the most physi-
cally capable or violent members of the criminal element,
but  they’re  useful  contacts  and  can  fill  the  role  of  street
toughs and gang members.

Thugs  can  be  used  as  muggers,  gangsters,  gang  mem-
bers,  and  henchmen  for  supervillains.  They  can  also  be
used  as  any  type  of  hired  muscle  from  a  bodyguard  to
security guards.
