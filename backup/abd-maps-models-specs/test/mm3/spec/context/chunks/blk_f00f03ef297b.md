LIBRARY

A library allows for use of various Knowledge skills when
doing  research.  A  library  may  consist  of  printed  matter
(books and periodicals), microfilm, digital files, or a combi-
nation of all three. A library may facilitate certain Expertise
skill checks and provide a circumstance bonus for them.
