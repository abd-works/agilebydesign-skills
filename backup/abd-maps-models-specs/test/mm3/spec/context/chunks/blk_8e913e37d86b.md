THE LIMITS OF EQUIPMENT

While equipment is useful it does have its limits, particu-
larly when compared to powers or devices. Equipment is
less  expensive—it’s  cheaper  to  have  a  handgun  than  a
Damage power or even a super-science blaster weapon—
but  equipment  is  also  more  limited.  Keep  the  following
limitations of equipment in mind.
