USING THIS BOOK

The  best  way  to  read  the  Hero’s  Handbook  depends  on
whether you plan to be a player or Gamemaster in your
MUTANTS & MASTERMINDS game. The Gamemaster creates the
world in which the heroes live and controls all non-player
characters  (NPCs)  such  as  thugs,  cops,  and  supervillains,
as well as the supporting cast. Each player controls a su-
perhero he or she has created, interacting with other play-
er characters as well as with the world and stories created
by the Gamemaster.
