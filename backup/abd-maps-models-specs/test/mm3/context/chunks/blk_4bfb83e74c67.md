ANIMALS

You can either speak to or comprehend animals. You can
ask questions and receive answers, although animals are
not  any  more  friendly  or  cooperative  than  normal.  Fur-
thermore, wary and cunning animals are likely to be terse
and  evasive,  while  especially  stupid  ones  make  inane
comments. If an animal is friendly toward you, it may do
some  favor  or  service  for  you.  For  2  ranks  you  can  both
speak to and understand the “speech” of animals.
