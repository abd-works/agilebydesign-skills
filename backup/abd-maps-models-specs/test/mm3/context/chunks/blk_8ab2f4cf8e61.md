ANIMAL EMPATHY

SKILL

You have a special connection with animals. You can use
interaction skills on animals normally, and do not have to
speak  a  language  the  animal  understands;  you  commu-
nicate  your  intent  through  gestures  and  body  language
and learn things by studying animal behavior. Characters
normally have a –10 circumstance penalty to use interac-
tion skills on animals, due to their Intellect and lack of lan-
guage.
