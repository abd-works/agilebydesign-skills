STANDARD ACTION

You attempt to damage or break an object held or worn
by  an  opponent.  Make  an  attack  check  against  the  de-
fense of the character with the object, with a –5 circum-
stance penalty if you are attacking a held object. If your
attack check succeeds, you inflict damage on the object
rather than the character. See Damaging Objects for de-
tails on breaking things.
