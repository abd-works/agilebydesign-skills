TRACKING

1 RANK

Innate: Senses, particularly those of aliens or constructs
like robots, may be Innate, although this does not prevent
sensory effects like Concealment or Dazzle from disabling
them. Flat +1 point.

You can follow trails and track using a particular sense. Ba-
sic  DC  to  follow  a  trail  is  10,  modified  by  circumstances,
as the GM sees fit. You move at your speed rank –1 while
tracking. For 2 ranks, you can move at full normal speed
while tracking

Ranged: Likewise, the Ranged extra only applies to Sens-
es that Affect Others, extending the distance at which you
can grant their benefits. To extend the range of a sense it-
self, use the Extended, Radius, and Ranged options of the
Senses effect. +1 or +2 cost per rank.
