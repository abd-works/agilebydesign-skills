PERMANENT

-1 COST PER RANK

A continuous effect with this flaw becomes permanent in
duration. It cannot be turned off, it is always on by default.
If some outside force—usually a Nullify effect—does turn
it off, it turns back on automatically at the earliest oppor-
tunity. Additionally, you cannot improve a permanent ef-
fect using extra effort.

Permanent effects may be inconvenient at times (includ-
ing things like being permanently incorporeal or 30 feet
tall); this is included in the value of the flaw.
