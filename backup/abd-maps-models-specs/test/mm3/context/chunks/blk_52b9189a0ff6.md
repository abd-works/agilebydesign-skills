ADVANTAGES

Choose One: Power Attack or Accurate Attack

Take the advantages listed above, then roll 1d20 once and
record the result.

1-8

Imposing: Your form is fearsome and impressive.

9-12

Overseer: You tend to take a guiding role.

13-20

Unobtrusive: You blend into the background and
stay out of the way.
