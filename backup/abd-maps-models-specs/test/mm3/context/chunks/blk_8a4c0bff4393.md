SPENDING POWER POINTS

Each  trait  costs  a  certain  number  of  power  points.  You
“spend” or allocate your points to give your character dif-
ferent traits. Once spent, power points cannot be re-allo-
cated without the use of a particular power or the Game-
master’s permission. The basic costs of traits are given on
the Basic Trait Costs table, with specific costs for powers
given in the Powers chapter.
