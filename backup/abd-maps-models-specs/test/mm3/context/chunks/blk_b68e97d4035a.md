MOVE ACTIONS

A  move  action,  like  the  name  implies,  usually  involves
moving.  You  can  take  your  move  action  before  or  after
your  standard  action,  so  you  can  attack  then  move,  or
move then attack. You cannot, however, normally split-up
your move action before and after your standard action.
Move  actions  also  include  things  like  drawing  weapons,
standing up, and picking up or manipulating objects.
