ABILITIES

Roll 1d20 once and record the result.

1-6

Charismatic: You have a powerful force of
personality.

7-14 Hotheaded: You are impetuous and reckless.

15-20 Reserved: You are aloof or serene.

16-20

Vapor Form: Roll for the powers of an Air Elemental
instead, using water vapor as your form’s descriptor.
