ACTION TYPES

The four types of actions characters can take are standard,
move, free, and reaction. In a normal round, you can per-
form a standard action and a move action, or two move
actions. You can also perform as many free and reactions
actions as your GM allows.

Some situations (like the surprise round) and conditions
(like  being  dazed)  limit  the  actions  you  can  take  during
your turn.
