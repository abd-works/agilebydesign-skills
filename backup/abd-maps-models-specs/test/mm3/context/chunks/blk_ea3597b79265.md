MEDIUM

A power’s medium is what the power uses to accomplish its
effect(s). Often, a power’s source and medium are one and
the  same:  a  psionic  power  uses  psionic  energy  to  power
and  accomplish  its  effects,  likewise,  a  divine  power  often
uses divine energy to power and accomplish its effects.

In  some  cases,  however,  source  and  medium  may  differ
and  the  distinction  may  be  significant.  For  example,  the
power  to  throw  fireballs  granted  by  the  God  of  Fire  is  a
bestowed origin with a divine source, using fire as the me-
dium to cause its Damage effect.

energy:  material  mediums  are  substances,  ranging  from
