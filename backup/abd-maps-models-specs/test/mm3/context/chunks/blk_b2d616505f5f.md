LETHALITY

Comics  have  different  levels  of  lethality  and  character
death. It’s important to make the expectations of your se-
ries clear to the players, just so there’s no misunderstand-
ing.  A  hero  who  refuses  to  kill  under  any  circumstances
might be an interesting oddity in a dark and gritty series
full  of  militaristic  super-agents,  but  a  cold-blooded  vigi-
lante in a four-color world of merciful, law-abiding heroes
