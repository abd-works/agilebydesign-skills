MELEE WEAPONS

Melee  weapons  are  hand-held  close  combat  weapons.
They typically have a Strength-based Damage effect (see
the  Damage  effect  in  the  Powers  chapter),  adding  the
wielder’s Strength rank to the weapon’s damage rank. Or-

exerts  Strength  greater  than  the  weapon’s  Toughness
