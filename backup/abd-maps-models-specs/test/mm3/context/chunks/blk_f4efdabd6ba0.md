KEY POINTS

The Fire of the Sun and Moon is a small statue about two
feet tall, carved of whalebone joined and inlaid with gold
mined from the Atlas Mountains to form a small arch. Bird-
like  humanoids  representing  the  Hekawi  brother-gods
Nimuatu the kestrel and Maquami the owl hold a golden
orb aloft in their wings as they keep watch over day and
night, respectively. Heroes with Expertise in mythology or
religion (or a hero who makes a Perception check (DC 5) to
read the signs nearby) know the legend in the “The Kestrel
and the Owl” sidebar.
