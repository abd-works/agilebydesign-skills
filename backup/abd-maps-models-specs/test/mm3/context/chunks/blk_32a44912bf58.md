ARCHAIC ARMOR

Some characters in superheroic settings still wear ancient
or archaic armor, either because they are from a place or
time where such armor is common or because it is tied to
their motif or powers in some way.

Leather: This can be archaic plates of boiled leather or a
modern heavy leather jacket.
