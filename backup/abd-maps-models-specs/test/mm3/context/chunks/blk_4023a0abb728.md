INSUBSTANTIAL DESCRIPTORS

Note  that  the  fluid,  gaseous,  etc.,  rank  names  are  them-
selves essentially descriptors for the different Insubstan-
tial effects. A character with Insubstantial 1 might instead
be  a  stretchable,  rubbery  form  rather  than  a  liquid,  for
example, while one with Insubstantial 2 could transform
into a swarm of insects rather than a gas.
