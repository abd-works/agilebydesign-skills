ENDOWMENT

Some outside force grants the hero powers. This might be
an experimental procedure (see the next entry), a godlike
higher power, a secret organization that hands out pow-
erful  devices,  a  mysterious  wizard,  or  something  similar.
The  patron  might  expect  something  in  return  from  the
hero for this boon, or the gift could be unconditional.
