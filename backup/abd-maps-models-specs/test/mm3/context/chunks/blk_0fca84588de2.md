BONUS STACKING

Equipment bonuses are limited compared to the bonuses
granted by other effects. Generally, they do not stack with
each  other  or  other  types  of  bonuses,  only  the  highest
bonus applies. Thus a hero with a high Protection bonus
doesn’t get much, if any, advantage from wearing a bul-
letproof vest. The only exception to this is Strength-based
weapons, and there are limits on them as well (see Melee
Weapons, later in this chapter).
