HEKAWI MYSTICS

These  members  of  the  Hekawi  tribe  live  on  reservations
outside Emerald City, and have maintained the old ways.
Their unabiding faith in their gods grants them access to
powerful magic they use to defend their people and their
beliefs.  They  aren’t  trained  combatants,  but  they  fight
zealously knowing their faith protects them.
