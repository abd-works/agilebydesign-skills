EVASION

COMBAT, RANKED (2)

You  have  a  +2  circumstance  bonus  to  Dodge  resistance
checks  to  avoid  area  effects  (see  the  Area  extra  in  the
Powers  chapter).  If  you  have  2  ranks  in  this  advantage,
your circumstance bonus increases to +5.
