OVERCOMING ILLUSIONS

Characters encountering an illusion do not receive checks
to recognize it as illusory until they interact with it in some
fashion. A successful Insight check against an illusion (DC
10 + Illusion rank) reveals it as false. A failed check means
the  character  does  not  notice  anything  amiss.  A  charac-
