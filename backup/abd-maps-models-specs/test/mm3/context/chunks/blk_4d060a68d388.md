WEATHER CONTROLLER

Weather  Controllers  combine  control  over  the  elements
of air and water with cold and electrical energy. They gen-
erally command the more violent aspects of the weather,
such as storms and blizzards. As such, Weather Controllers
wield an impressive array of offensive powers that allow
them to strike groups of targets from range.

Roll 1d20 once and record the result.

1-7

8-14

Conjurer: You appeal to the forces of nature to grant
you your powers.

Psionic: You control the weather through force of
will.

15-20

Technological: You use scientific means to
manipulate the weather.
