TACTICS

To  Simian,  his  plan  is  eminently  reasonable,  and  in  all  his
exchanges with the heroes he emphasizes the cold logic of
mutual  necessity.  Having  correctly  deduced  both  Marmo-
Set and Choshech’s weaknesses, Simian is supremely, cooly
confident  the  super-humans  will  fail  without  him  and  be
forced to meet his terms, now or soon after. He is unarmed,
has nothing to gain from a fight, and believes time is on his
side, so Simian exercises only his wits throughout this scene.
