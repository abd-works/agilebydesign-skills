TACTICS

The Sons of the Sky have a common cause, using the Fire
of the Sun and Moon in their long-planned ritual. But in
battle,  they  don’t  form  a  very  good  partnership.  Avian
Sons tend to keep their part of the fight in the skies, using
weaponry  brought  down  from  their  homes  in  dormant
volcanoes  high  in  the  mountains.  Meanwhile,  Hekawi
mystics attempt to hold the ground using magical  abili-
ties they believe come from the source of all wisdom, the
river goddess Faloshenka.

Throughout the battle the Avian warriors will attempt to
draw the heroes away from the ritual, preferring to engage
them in the air if possible where they can take advantage
of  their  skilled  maneuverability. The  Hekawi  mystics  will
continue their ritual rather than fight.
