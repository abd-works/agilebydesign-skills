CONSTRUCT

Constructs cover a broad range of character types,  from
robots  to  golems  or  animated  statues  to  undead.  Re-
ally, this includes anything that moves, but doesn’t have
a  physical  or  mental  makeup  quite  like  a  human’s.  Con-
structs tend to be physically powerful, resilient, and often
have some sort of ranged attack that’s either a weapon or
innate ability granted by their inhuman form. Most Con-
structs are solid, but it’s possible to have a Construct made
of fire, water, or just about anything else.
