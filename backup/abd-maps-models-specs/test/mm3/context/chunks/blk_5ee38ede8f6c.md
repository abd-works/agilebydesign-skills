GETTING THE HEROES INVOLVED

Depending on how the heroes are organized, they can get
involved in the adventure in a few different ways.

•

If the heroes are friendly with the authorities, the police
contact them and mention a gang of supervillains is at-
tacking the Pyramid Plaza buildings (the city’s tallest).
