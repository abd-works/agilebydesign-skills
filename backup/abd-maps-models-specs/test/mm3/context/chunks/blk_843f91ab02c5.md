ABILITIES

Roll 1d20 once and record the result.

1-4

Golem: You were literally assembled in some
way. You’re a golem, living statue, some sort
of elemental creature, or a human who’s been
transformed into a “non-living” but animate form.

5-12

Technological: You’re a robot, android, or some
other sort of technological construct.

13-20

Undead: You died, but you’ve been brought back
to (some semblance of ) life as a zombie, ghost,
revenant, or another form of undead.
