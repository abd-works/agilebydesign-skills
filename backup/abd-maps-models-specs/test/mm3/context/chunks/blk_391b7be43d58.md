SWINGING

You  can  swing  through  the  air  at  your  normal  ground
speed  rank,  using  a  swing-line  you  provide  or  available
lines  and  projections  (tree  limbs,  flagpoles,  vines,  tele-
phone- and power-lines, etc.).
