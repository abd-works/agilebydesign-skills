PLAYERS

If you’re creating a hero for a MUTANTS & MASTERMINDS game,
take a look through the Secret Origins chapter for a basic
overview. The following chapters contain all the informa-
tion you need to create your own hero. You may want to
consult with your Gamemaster before creating a hero to
find out what sort of series your GM is interested in run-
ning, and what types of heroes are appropriate.
