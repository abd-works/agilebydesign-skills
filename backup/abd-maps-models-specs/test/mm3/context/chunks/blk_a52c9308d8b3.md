ACTION ROUNDS

The action round (or simply round) is how MUTANTS & MASTERMINDS breaks down time when things like who goes first
and how much each character can accomplish are important. A round represents about six seconds of time in the
game world.

During a round, each character involved takes a turn, which is that character’s opportunity to do something. A character
has an allotment of actions, used during that character’s turn. Players decide what their characters do on their turns,
while the GM handles everyone else’s turn.
