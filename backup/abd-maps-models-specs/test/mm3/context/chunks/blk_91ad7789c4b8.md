PHYSICAL SKILLS

Roll 1d20 once and record the result.

1-6

Flamboyant: You fight with great flair.

7-14

Instinctive: You let your well-honed reflexes take over.

15-20

Sneaky: You prefer to avoid a direct confrontation.
