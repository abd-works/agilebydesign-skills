AVAILABILITY

Ownership of some equipment is restricted and the GM
decides what is available in the setting. For example, guns
may  require  permits,  licenses,  waiting  periods,  and  so
forth.  Also,  equipment  can  be  bulky  and  difficult  to  car-
ry  around.  Gamemasters  are  encouraged  to  enforce  the
limitations of carrying a lot of equipment at once. Players
