UNIVERSAL

At this scale, Earth is just one small part of the vast uni-
verse,  and  the  heroes  regularly  travel  among  the  stars.
This is the scale for cosmic heroes such as an interstellar
police force, or for far-future teams that patrol a solar sys-
tem and beyond, where it’s handling a natural disaster on
one  planet,  quickly  followed  by  an  invasion  of  another
world in a different star system. Some form of Space Travel
(see Movement, page 172) is required at this scale.
