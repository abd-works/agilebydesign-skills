## Scenario walkthrough: object flow is trace-complete

Each **Walk** must carry an **object flow** from entry to exit: actors/concepts, handoffs, and at least one **Covers** line that states what collaboration is demonstrated.

**DO**

- End each walk with a clear outcome or explicit **gap** (see **scenario-walkthrough-update-spec-on-gap**).

**DON'T**

- List concepts without a ordered flow when the story is substantive.
