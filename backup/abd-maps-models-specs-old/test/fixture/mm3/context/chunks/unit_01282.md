## VEHICLES

Dexterity • Trained Only • Manipulation Use this skill to operate vehicles, from ground vehicles like cars to boats, planes, or even spaceships! See Vehicles in the Gadgets & Gear chapter for details. Routine tasks, such as ordinary operation of known vehi- cles, don’t require a check and may even be done untrained for some vehicles, particularly common ones like cars. Make a check only when operating the vehicle in a stressful or dramatic situation like being chased or attacked, or trying to reach a destination in a limited amount of time.
