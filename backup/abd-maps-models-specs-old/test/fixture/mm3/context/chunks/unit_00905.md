## SKILL BENCHMARKS

You can get a general idea of just how good a particu- lar character’s skill bonus is using the general difficulty class guidelines given in The Basics along with the rules for routine checks (see Routine Checks in that chapter). For example, a +5 total skill modifier means the char- acter can routinely achieve a result of 15 (a tough task). Safe to say the character is a pro, able to routinely han- dle tasks that would prove too much for someone less skilled. A character with a +10 skill modifier achieve a DC 20 (challenging task) on a routine basis, a real level of expertise, while a +15 modifier can routinely complete DC 25 (formidable) tasks. At the high end, a +30 skill modifier can routinely accomplishing the nigh impos- sible (DC 40 tasks)!
