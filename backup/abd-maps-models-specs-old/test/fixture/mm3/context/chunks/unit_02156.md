## ILLUSORY APPEARANCE

Illusion can alter a subject’s appearance, providing an essentially impenetrable disguise—at least until some- one makes a successful check to see through the illu- sion. However, for just the ability to alter appearance, use the Morph effect, which is generally more effective than Illusion Limited to Appearance.
