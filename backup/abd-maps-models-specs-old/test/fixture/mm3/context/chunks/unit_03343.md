Effect: The effect a hit with the weapon causes, typically Damage, although some modern melee weapons have other effects. The effect has the normal cost given in the Powers chapter. The effect may also have certain descrip- tors, such as bludgeoning or slashing, for defining things like resistance or vulnerability to certain effects. Critical: The threat range for a critical hit with the weap- on. Some weapons have a larger threat range than others. Increasing a weapon’s threat range by 1 costs 1 point, like the Improved Critical advantage. Cost: This is the weapon’s cost in points. Characters pay this cost from their equipment points to have a weapon of this type as part of their regular equipment.
