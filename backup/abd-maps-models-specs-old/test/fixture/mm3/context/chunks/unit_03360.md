## RANGED WEAPONS

Ranged weapons include both thrown and projectile weapons. Thrown weapons are Strength-based, adding the wielder’s Strength rank to their Damage rank. Projec- tile weapons include bows, crossbows, and guns as well as energy weapons like lasers and blasters. Their Damage is generally not Strength-based. Like melee weapons, ranged weapons have category, ef- fect, critical, and cost traits. Ranged weapon categories are Projectile Weapons, Energy Weapons, Heavy Weap- ons, and Thrown Weapons.
