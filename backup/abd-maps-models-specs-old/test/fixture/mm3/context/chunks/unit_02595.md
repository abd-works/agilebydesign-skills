## FLAWS

Attitude: Your summoned minions are less than coopera- tive: indifferent or even unfriendly. You can use interaction skills and other effects to get your summoned minions to cooperate, but success is by no means assured! Indifferent: –1 cost per rank. Unfriendly: –2 cost per rank. Resistible: Your minions get an appropriate resistance check (typically Will) against (DC 10 + Summon rank) to avoid being summoned. If they successfully resist, you cannot attempt to summon them again in that scene without using extra effort. –1 cost per rank.
