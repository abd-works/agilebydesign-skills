## DELUXE HERO’S HANDBOOK

Characters are assumed to be literate in their native language and any other language they know. At the GM’s discretion, characters may have to spend an additional Languages rank to be literate in a language with a different alphabet or style of writing from the character’s native language (such as Arabic, Japanese kanji or Russian Cyrillic for an English speaker). Completely illiterate characters are faced with a potential Disability complication during the game. level limits, like other uses of hero points. Multiple uses of Inspire do not stack, only the highest bonus applies.
