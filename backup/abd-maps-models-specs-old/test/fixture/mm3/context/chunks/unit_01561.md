## FORTUNE, RANKED (1/2 PL)

Once per round, you can choose to re-roll a die roll, like spending a hero point (see Hero Points, page 20), includ- ing adding 10 to re-rolls of 10 or less. You can do this a number of times per game session equal to your Luck rank, with a maximum rank of half the series power level (rounded down). Your Luck ranks refresh when your hero points “reset” at the start of an adventure. The GM may choose to set a different limit on ranks in this advantage, depending on the series. Generally speaking, languages are not terribly important in comic book superhero stories except as background col- or or occasional plot complications. Gamemasters should allow players with characters fluent in other languages the occasional opportunity to show them off or put them to good use. If you specifically set up the language barrier as an obstacle by confronting the heroes with a language they cannot possibly understand, that should count as a complication and be worth a hero point.
