## 2. GAMEMASTER GUIDELINES

Your GM may have particular guidelines for characters in the game, such as not allowing certain powers or concepts or requiring particular descriptors. If there are no aliens in the setting, for example, then you obviously can’t play an alien hero. Likewise, if your Gamemaster bans mental powers from the series, then a psychic isn’t appropriate. Run your hero concept by your Gamemaster before you start working on it! You might also want to consult with your fellow players so you can design your characters to- gether and ensure they’ll make a good team.
