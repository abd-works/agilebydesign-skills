## EQUIPMENT EFFECTS AND FEATURES

An item’s cost is based on its effects and features, just like a power (see the Powers chapter for more informa- tion), so a ranged weapon has a cost based on its Ranged Damage rank. Equipment often provides the Features effect, including some specific equipment Features de- scribed in this chapter. Indeed, some items of equipment provide only Features.
