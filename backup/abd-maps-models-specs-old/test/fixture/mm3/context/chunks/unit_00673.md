## REAL NAME

Some heroes go by their given name, not using a code- name at all. Oftentimes these names still sound like code- names, however. They may also be nicknames, such as “Dash” for someone with the name Dashell, or “Buzz” for someone with the name Buzcinski, or whatever other nickname a character may have, such as “Stretch” or “Tiny”.
