## RESISTANCE

A successful attack has some effect on the target. Typical- ly this is an effect from the Powers chapter, such as Dam- age or Affliction. The effect has a rank, used to determine a difficulty class for the target’s resistance check. Resistance Difficulty = effect rank + 10 The target of the attack makes a resistance check against the effect to determine what, if anything, happens.
