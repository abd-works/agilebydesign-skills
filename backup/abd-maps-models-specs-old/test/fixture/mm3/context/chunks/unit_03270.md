## TECHNOLOGICAL LIMITS

Equipment includes only items and technology commonly available in the setting. The GM decides what is “commonly available,” but as a rule of thumb assume equipment only includes things from the real world, not battlesuits, anti- gravity devices, shrink rays, and so forth. Those are all de- vices (see Devices at the start of this chapter).
