## HEAVY WEAPONS

Flamethrower: A flamethrower shoots a stream or arc of fire Damage as Cone or Line Area and can switch between settings as an Alternate Effect. Grenade launcher: A grenade launcher fires various types of grenades out a greater distance, generally frag- mentation grenades. Rocket launcher: A rocket-launcher is generally an anti- tank weapon, although they also make useful anti-super- hero weapons as well.
