## CHANGING COMPLICATIONS

Complications can (and generally should) change over the course of a series: old enemies die or are put away for good, rivalries and psychological issues are resolved, new ro- mances and relationships begin as others end, and so forth. Work with the GM to come up with new complications for your hero as old ones are resolved. As mentioned previous- ly, the Gamemaster may set limits as to how many ongoing complications your hero can have in play at any given time.
