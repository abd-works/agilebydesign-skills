## OTHER DEVICES

The full range of devices MUTANTS & MASTERMINDS characters can create and use is limited solely by your imagination. Essentially any item with a power is considered a device. Players and GMs may well come up with devices beyond those described here. Use the guidelines in this chapter and in the Powers chapter to handle any new devices and their capabilities.
