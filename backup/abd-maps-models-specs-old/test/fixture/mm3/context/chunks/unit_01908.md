## OBJECTS

You can communicate with inanimate objects, grant- ing them the ability to speak to you or simply “reading” impressions from them. This requires two Comprehend ranks. Objects only “know” about events directly affecting them or occurring in their immediate area. Gamemasters can apply the guidelines for Postcognition (see page 178) to this effect.
