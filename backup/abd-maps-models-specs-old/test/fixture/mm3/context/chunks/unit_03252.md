## DAMAGING EQUIPMENT

Most equipment can be damaged like other objects (see Damaging Objects, page 244), based on its Toughness. Equipment suffering damage loses some effectiveness. The item loses 1 Feature or suffers a –1 circumstance pen- alty on checks involving it each time it is damaged. These penalties are eliminated once the item is repaired.
