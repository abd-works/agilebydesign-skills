## WATER VEHICLES

Water vehicles range from small boats and outboards to massive sea-going ships. Cutters are used by the Coast Guard and the Navy. They’re often equipped with light machine guns (Ranged Multiat- tack Damage 6). Destroyers are main naval ships, carrying heavy guns (Ranged Damage 10, Burst Area 8). Battleships have massive gun batteries (Ranged Damage 13, Burst Area 9) and heavy armor. Submarines are equipped with torpedoes (Ranged Dam- age 8, Burst Area 5) and often ballistic missiles (Ranged Burst Area Damage 15 or higher, not included in listed cost).
