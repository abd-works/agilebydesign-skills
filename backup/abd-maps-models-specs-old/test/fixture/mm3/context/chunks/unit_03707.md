## SELF-REPAIRING

The structure of the headquarters “heals” any damage done to it over time. Essentially, it recovers like a character The only exception to the Alternate Headquarters guide- line is a shared headquarters (previously), the characters sharing the HQ all contribute to its cost and pay for any personal headquarters separately. 230230
