## ENVIRONMENTAL ADAPTATION

You’re adapted to a particular environment, such as un- derwater, zero gravity, and so forth (see Environmental Hazards, pages 185-187, for details). You suffer none of the normal unfavorable circumstance or movement pen- alties associated with that environment, moving and act- ing normally. You are still affected by environmental haz- ards like suffocation, exposure, and so forth. You need the Immunity effect to ignore such things.
