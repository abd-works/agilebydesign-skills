## TOUGHNESS

Toughness defense is based on Stamina and is resistance to direct damage or harm, and overall durability. Your base defense ranks are equal to your ranks in their associated abilities. You can increase your defenses above the values granted by your ability ranks by spending pow- er points: 1 power point grants you an additional rank in a defense, up to the limits imposed by power level (see Power Level on page 24). Defense Cost = 1 power point per +1 rank With the Enhanced Trait effect (see the Powers chapter) you can also improve your defenses with powers at the same cost, 1 point per rank. 110110
