## MENTAL BLAST

Effect: Perception Ranged Damage, Resisted by Will • 4 points per rank You can strike targets’ minds with “mental force,” inflict- ing Damage resisted by the target’s Will rather than Toughness, but having no effect on targets immune to effects resisted by Will, such as inanimate objects. Men- tal Blasts are often, but not always, Subtle as well, which costs a flat 1 point.
