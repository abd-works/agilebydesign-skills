## TASK

Diagnose injuries and ailments. Provide long-term care. Revive dazed or stunned characters. Stabilize dying character. Treat diseases or poisons. If you do not have the appropriate medical equipment and supplies, you take a –5 circumstance penalty on your check. If your subject has a particularly unusual biology (an alien, for example) you may also suffer a circumstance penalty. You can use the Treatment skill on yourself, but only to diagnose, provide care, or treat disease or poison. You take a –5 circumstance penalty on checks when treating yourself.
