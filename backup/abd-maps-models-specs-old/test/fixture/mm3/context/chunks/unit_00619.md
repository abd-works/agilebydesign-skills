## MOTIVATIONS AS ORIGINS

Some characters may derive their powers from their mo- tivation in some way, such as heroes who draw strength from their convictions, faith, or morality. This provides a descriptor for those powers, but the hero may also suffer Power Loss (see page 28) from a change or wavering in motivation.
