## POWERS

Constructs can have various power effects, like other char- acters. Some effects are less useful or even useless to con- structs and the GM has final say as to whether or not a particular effect can be assigned to a construct. Power ef- fects are often aspects of a construct’s makeup or design, such as built-in armor (Protection), weapons (Damage), or sensors (Sense).
