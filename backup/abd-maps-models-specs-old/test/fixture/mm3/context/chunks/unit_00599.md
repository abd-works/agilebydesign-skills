## MOTIVATION

Every hero has something that drove him or her to be- come a hero in the first place—a motivation that keeps them going when things get tough. Sometimes motiva- tion is the only difference between a hero and a villain. What made your hero decide to fight for justice rather than turning toward more selfish goals? How does it affect the hero’s methods of fighting crime? Is there anything that might change or affect the hero’s motivation? Motivation is a complication because it often determines what a hero will do in a particular situation. The GM can use your hero’s motivation to encourage certain actions, and enemies may do the same.
