## GROUNDS

In addition to the actual building(s) of the headquarters, it has a considerable area of land surrounding it. An HQ can have surrounding land of one size category larger than the structure at no cost, without having this feature. Hav- ing it allows for grounds up to three size categories larger than the structure, so a large mansion headquarters could have a colossal area of land. If the headquarters has features like Defense System and Security System, they also extend over the grounds (with fences, sensors, weapon emplacements, and so forth).
