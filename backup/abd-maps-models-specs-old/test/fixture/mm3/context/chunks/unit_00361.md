## ATTACK CHECKS

An attack check determines whether or not you hit an opponent in combat with an attack. It is a d20 roll plus your bonus with that particular attack, usually based off of Fighting or Dexterity and appropriate modifiers, like the Close and Ranged Combat skills. The difficulty is your target’s defense class: Parry for close attacks, Dodge for ranged attacks. Certain attacks may target other defenses. If you equal or exceed your target’s defense class result, your attack hits. Otherwise, you miss. Attack Check = d20 + attack bonus + modifiers vs. defense class A natural 20 on an attack check (where the die comes up 20) always hits and may be a critical hit (see Critical Hits in the Action & Adventure chapter). A natural 1 on an attack check (where the die comes up 1) always misses, regardless of the check total. This differs from normal checks and re- flects the variable and unpredictable nature of combat.
