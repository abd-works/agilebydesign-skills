## ENHANCED EQUIPMENT

Some devices are otherwise normal equipment with spe- cial properties. Magical items, normal equipment imbued with magical properties, are examples. Magical weap- ons may have greater damage bonuses or grant attack bonuses while magical armor imposes no penalties and provides greater protection. Such enchantments move archaic weapons and armor from the realm of mundane equipment to devices. The same is true of equipment us- ing super-alloys, bulletproof cloth, and other wonders of super-science.
