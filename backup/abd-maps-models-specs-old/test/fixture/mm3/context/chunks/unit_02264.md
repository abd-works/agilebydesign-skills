## MIND CONTROL

Ranged: Luck Control normally requires no attack check; if Ranged, it does. –1 cost per rank. Resistible: Targets of your Luck Control get a resistance check—usually Dodge or Will—to avoid its effects. –1 cost per rank. Side Effect: As a particular side effect of Luck Control, if your effort to alter luck fails, you suffer a setback with- out earning a hero point. Effectively the GM gains a “free” complication against you. –1 or –2 cost per rank.
