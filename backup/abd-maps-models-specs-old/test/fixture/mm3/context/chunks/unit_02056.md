## VISIBILITY

You impose a -2 modifier to Perception checks for 1 point per rank, and a -5 for 2 points per rank. For more signifi- cant obscuring of senses (via darkness, fog, etc.) use an Area Concealment Attack effect (see Concealment in this chapter).
