## DOCK

A dock houses water vehicles and includes access to a nearby waterway, an airlock or lock system for moving ve- hicles in and out of the dock, and dry-dock facilities for re- pairing and maintaining water vehicles. The headquarters should be located within reasonable distance of a body of water to have this feature.
