## ENHANCED ABILITIES

Some ability ranks—or portions of them—may be ac- quired as Enhanced Traits, as described in the Powers chapter. Enhanced Abilities are superhuman powers rath- er than natural. The key differences between Enhanced Abilities and normal ability ranks are Enhanced Abilities can be nullified (normal abilities cannot, see Nullify, page 173) and Enhanced Abilities can have power modifiers and be used for power stunts with extra effort (normal abilities cannot, see Extra Effort, page 19). Enhanced Abilities and normal abilities have the same cost (2 power points per +1 ability rank).
