## DC

0 5 10 15 20 –10 –5 –5 +2 +5 +5 +5 Since you can’t easily move to avoid attacks, you are vul- nerable while climbing unless you accept a +5 increase in the DC. Any time you fail a resistance check while climb- ing, make an immediate Athletics check against the DC of the climb. Failure means you fall. At the GM’s discretion, certain kinds of climbing attempts might require tools like ropes, pitons, harness, and so forth. Attempting such a climb without tools incurs a –5 circumstance penalty.
