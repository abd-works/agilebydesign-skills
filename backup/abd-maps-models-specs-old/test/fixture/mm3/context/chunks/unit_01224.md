## REPAIRING

You can also use Technology to repair damaged items, with a –5 to the DC to build the item and –2 to the time rank required. So you can perform repairs on a complex item in eight hours (time rank 12) with a DC of 20. Failure on the check means you spend the time, but make no progress. Two or more degrees of failure may indicate further dam- age to the item or an accident similar to building it. You can reduce the time rank to build or repair an item by 1 by taking a –5 penalty to your skill check.
