## FLAWS

The following section lists available flaws, starting with the flaw’s name and the amount it reduces effect cost (in power points per rank or flat value), along with a descrip- tion of how the flaw modifies effects in game terms. A flat-value flaw cannot have more ranks than the effect itself.
