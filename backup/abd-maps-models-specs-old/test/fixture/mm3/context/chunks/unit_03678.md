## LIVING SPACE

The headquarters includes all the necessary amenities for people to live there full-time. This is usually a number of residents equal to the HQ’s power level comfortably (pos- sibly more, at the GM’s discretion). It includes bedrooms or private suites, kitchen facilities, dining area, and com- mon living areas. Characters can live in a headquarters lacking this feature short-term, but they’re not likely to be very comfortable.
