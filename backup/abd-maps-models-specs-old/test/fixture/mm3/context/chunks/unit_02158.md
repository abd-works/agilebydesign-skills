## MENTAL ILLUSIONS

The default Illusion effect is perceptible to anyone or anything (including machines) as if it were real. Some illusions exist solely in the mind, like projected psychic hallucinations. This type of Illusion has the Resistible by Will flaw and the Selective extra, since the illusionist can choose whether or not to project the illusion into a par- ticular subject’s mind, and therefore decides who can or cannot perceive the illusion. This is a net +0 modifier, for the same base cost.
