## POWERS

Powers are special abilities beyond those of ordinary hu- man beings. They’re like advantages, only much more so. Whereas an advantage might give your hero a minor spe- cial ability, powers grant truly superhuman abilities. Those abilities are based on effects, which describe what a power does in game terms. A power may have just one effect or several, and you can apply various modifiers to the effects to change how they work, customizing them to get just the right power. Power effects have ranks like abilities do, on a scale from 1 to 20 (sometimes more). Unlike abilities, effects do not have ranks of less than 1, since the “average” is not having powers at all! Some power effects require checks to use, while others operate automatically. For full descriptions of the various effects powers can have, see the Powers chapter.
